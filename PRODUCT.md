# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who want a private AI companion experience shaped like a small personal phone, not a generic chat website. They open it to continue a relationship, review shared moments, or adjust personal and character settings.

## Product Purpose

The product presents a virtual phone prototype with four fixed desktops: Today, Companion, Memories, and Manage. Success means the user can understand the information architecture, open every parent and child feature window, try chat and call mock flows, switch themes, and experience the intended interaction rhythm without connecting real AI, storage, auth, payment, notifications, or media generation.

## Brand Personality

Restrained, modular, intimate.

## Anti-references

Do not make it a normal SaaS landing page, admin dashboard, app grid, game menu, neon/glass interface, saturated colorful UI, or a generic AI chat page. Avoid extra pages beyond the four required desktops, mixed icon systems, emoji as functional icons, dense cards, heavy shadows, and real backend behavior.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the four-page phone structure as the core product model.
- Make relationship context visible through status, recent activity, memories, and gentle prompts.
- Keep management actions centralized in the fourth page.
- Use mock states honestly: every unfinished capability opens a clear, user-facing placeholder with a return path.
- Favor geometric order, whitespace, and calm motion over decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast, keyboard navigation for page switching, visible focus states, responsive phone sizing from small mobile to desktop, and `prefers-reduced-motion` support for all animated effects.
